# Press Release

## GI‑SMART Launches Global AI Education Programs Across Four Continents

**FOR IMMEDIATE RELEASE**

---

**Paris, France** — The Global Institute for Strategic Management & Advanced Research Technologies (GI‑SMART) has officially announced the global launch of two groundbreaking programs: the Bachelor of Artificial Intelligence Design (BAID) and the Master of Science in Artificial Intelligence & Strategic Data Systems (AI‑SMART). These programs redefine higher education for the intelligent age, combining academic excellence, cross‑continental mobility, and real‑world industry engagement.

---

## Reimagining Education for the AI Era

GI‑SMART's programs are built on a vision to prepare students for the most in‑demand and transformative careers of the 21st century. Both BAID and AI‑SMART are designed to cultivate professionals who can combine creativity, strategy, and technical expertise to shape the future of artificial intelligence, design, and global innovation ecosystems.

---

## About the Programs

### The Bachelor of Artificial Intelligence Design (BAID)

The Bachelor of Artificial Intelligence Design (BAID) is a four‑year interdisciplinary degree blending AI, design thinking, and human‑computer interaction. Students learn to imagine, prototype, and deploy intelligent systems that enrich human experience.

### The Master of Science in Artificial Intelligence & Strategic Data Systems (AI‑SMART)

The Master of Science in Artificial Intelligence & Strategic Data Systems (AI‑SMART) is an 18‑month advanced program that integrates AI engineering, data analytics, robotics, and strategic leadership to prepare graduates for executive‑level roles in data‑driven organizations.

---

## A Global Academic Network

GI‑SMART operates through a four‑campus model — **Paris, Dubai, Germany, and Bucharest** — offering students a truly global experience. Learners can choose an 'Anchor Campus' as their home base and complete additional terms at the other campuses, building cultural fluency, professional networks, and regional experience across continents.
